Who Is The Audience for the Data Visualizations?

The primary audience is for student athletes as they seek where to go to school and have the highest chance of 1st Round Draft Selection on the next level.


What Type of Data Visualizations?

The Data Visualizations include the NFL 1st Round Draft Picks by College, Draft Picks by Conference, Team Picks By Conference, Team Picks by Position and NF Team Picks by Program.

Information about the conferences can be found at the following site: NFL Interactive Draft Picks By Conference Chart.
